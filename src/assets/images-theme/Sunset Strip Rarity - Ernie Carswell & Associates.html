Sunset Strip Rarity
1423 Oriole Drive
Sunset Strip, CA 90069

1423 Oriole sits on over 1.2 acres of private land, accessed by a gated and long private driveway -- a rarity in the Sunset Strip. Next to the driveway gates stands a Guard House station to monitor guests entering the compound. Court Ordered Sale, please request information from Broker. Photos are no longer current but represent the property as it was originally presented in 2012. Property will need full restoration. Sq footage quoted from Public Records, add'l sq footage may exist as separate Guest House and separate Spa complex, plus the 4 car Glass -Walled Garage structure. 1423 Oriole is positioned to once again become the crowning achievement in the Bird Streets upon completion of all necessary improvements and restoration by an expert development team. The original 2012 sale of this property at almost $40 Million began the Bird Streets' walk to fame among LA's preferred residential neighborhoods. Unprecedented.
